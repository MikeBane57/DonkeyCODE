Extension toolbar icons are generated from one master image.

Default in this repo:
  DonkeyCode/icons/donkey-logo.svg  →  rendered to source.png  →  icon16/32/48/128.png

Regenerate after editing the SVG or replacing the artwork:

1. Optional: replace DonkeyCode/icons/source.png with your own PNG (donkey, airplane, etc.).
   The image should read clearly at 16×16 (simple shapes help).

2. From the repo root:
   pip install -r DonkeyCode/scripts/requirements.txt
   python3 DonkeyCode/scripts/build-icons.py
   (Or: python3 DonkeyCode/scripts/build-icons.py /path/to/your-master.png)

   To rebuild source.png from the SVG only:
   python3 -c "import cairosvg; cairosvg.svg2png(url='DonkeyCode/icons/donkey-logo.svg', write_to='DonkeyCode/icons/source.png', output_width=512, output_height=512)"

3. Reload the extension on chrome://extensions (click the refresh icon on the card).
